Bio:

   

My name is Cassandra Campbell. I’m from the city that is known as “the city that never sleeps." That’s right. NYC! I’m from Brooklyn, NY. My birthday is December 1, 1994. I’m a senior at Spelman College majoring in Computer Science. I love to travel and explore new places.

 

Why Computer Science?

My major is Computer Science. I chose to major in this field because growing up I was the only girl out of four boys (my brother and three cousins) and I would often play video games with them. I enjoyed the field of technology so much that as I matured I decided to attend a Career and Technical Education high school where my concentration was Cisco Networking in Information Technology. In this course, I learned how to build internet networks, connect routers, make Ethernet cables, as well as the correlation between Domain Name Server and IP addresses. Once I entered this class, this is where I gained a passion for technology. My overall goal is to take a page out of my childhood book of when I played video games and create educational applications and video games for students in grades K-12.
